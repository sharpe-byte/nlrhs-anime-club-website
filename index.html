Information

The NLRHS Anime Club is a specialized literature club devoted to the friendly exchange of knowledge about Japan, Anime, Manga, Comics, Video-games and the Internet, as well as the culture surrounding it. We will dabble in a bit of everything from watching anime episodes to playing hit video games, to discussing all the latest news within the realm of anime fandom, and playing anime-inspired card games (Yu-Gi-Oh, Pokemon).
